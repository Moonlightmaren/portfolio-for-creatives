Mockingbird

Branding

Advertising

Illustration

Nearly half of Canadians aged 19–35 are choosing to limit or avoid alcohol, yet most nightlife spaces still revolve around drinking. As a result, sober and sober-curious people are often left feeling pressured, uncomfortable, or excluded from the social experience.

This alcohol-free bar reimagines nightlife by blending the openness of non-alcoholic spaces with the energy and sociability of a bar. Mockingbird creates a place where everyone can connect, proving that the best nights aren’t defined by what’s in your glass, but by who you share them with.

  • The Branding

    Bright, bold, and unapologetic, Mockingbird’s visual world reflects a nightlife experience that feels fresh, clear, and creatively charged. It’s vibrant but grounded—all the fun of a night out, without the negatives of alcohol.

    Tone of voice was key when marketing to a younger audience. The copy was crafted to strike a balance between witty and fun without trying too hard, while still clearly marketing the concept of a non-alcoholic bar.

  • Borrowed Notes, New Song

    Like how a mockingbird mimics the sounds of other birds, Mockingbird takes the best parts of a night out grabbing drinks with friends and aims to recreate that same experience—only without the hangover. The menu is inspired by the iconic alcoholic drinks we know and love, reinvented to be non-alcoholic.
