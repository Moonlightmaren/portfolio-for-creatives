Urban living can be isolating: nearly 40% of city dwellers report feeling lonely, despite being surrounded by people.

The Major 2nd Jazz Festival offers a cozy escape. Live music fosters real connection, celebrating individuality and shared experiences. Glowing windows across the city symbolize this warmth—each person and note telling a unique story that forms a vibrant, interconnected whole. The festival invites both jazz enthusiasts and newcomers to celebrate legendary music through heartfelt tribute performances.

  • The Major Second

    The major second interval, a key feature in many commonly used jazz scales, is where the festival gets its name.
