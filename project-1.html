Teens today are more connected than ever, yet 80% aren’t getting enough movement. Traditional workouts feel boring, exclusive, or intimidating, leaving few spaces that genuinely motivate them.

Checkpoint changes that. It’s a physical, video game–inspired park where fitness feels like adventure. Built on the same systems that make games engaging, leveling up, unlocking rewards, completing quests, and teaming up on missions, Checkpoint makes movement social, creative, and rewarding. Through bold design, and play-based progression, it transforms exercise into something teens actually want to do, a real-world checkpoint to connect, grow, and level up together

  • Graphics

    Checkpoint’s graphics blend retro and modern gaming art styles, giving the brand a distinctive look that sets it apart from other gyms and active brands while staying fresh and relevant for a younger audience.

  • Level Up

    Inspired by classic video game rewards, Checkpoint lets members earn cards that unlock perks, like merchandise and event access, based on their activity and achievements. Just scan your points card and start earning.

  • Play. Earn. Collect.

    Like video games keep players engaged with rewards, new gear, or a shiny sword, Checkpoint motivates teens through consistent challenges and rewards. Visitors can earn merchandise—like t-shirts or water bottles—based on how often they visit and the new challenges they complete. Mastering one of the park’s four areas earns them a collectible patch.
