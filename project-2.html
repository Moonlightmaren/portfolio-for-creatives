Northeast Scotland is often overlooked by travelers, overshadowed by destinations like the Isle of Skye or Edinburgh, despite its striking landscapes and rich heritage.

Firth Fragrance was born from a desire to capture the region’s quiet beauty in a way that feels personal and memorable. Named after the tidal estuaries that shape the Northeast coast, the brand creates unisex perfumes inspired by granite architecture, fresh coastal air, and rolling countryside of the region. Made sustainably with local artisans, Firth turns scent into a story, allowing visitors to take a piece of the Northeast home with them.

  • Packaging

    Each scent draws from a defining part of Northeast Scotland: the character of its coastal fishing towns, the calm of its windswept moors, and the strength of its granite architecture. A short description on each box highlights the fragrance notes and the inspiration behind them.
