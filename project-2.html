Northeast Scotland is often overlooked, overshadowed by destinations like the Isle of Skye or Edinburgh, despite its striking landscapes and rich heritage.

Firth Fragrance was born to capture the region’s quiet beauty in a personal, memorable way—a unique travel souvenir that transports you back to your journey. Inspired by the Northeast, the brand celebrates the area, encourages exploration, and highlights its local charm. Named after the tidal estuaries that shape the coast, Firth offers unisex perfumes drawn from granite architecture, fresh coastal air, and rolling countryside. Made sustainably with local artisans, each scent tells a story, letting visitors take a piece of the Northeast home.

Scroll to read through the Case study.

  • Elegance Meets Character

    Firth’s branding draws from Scottish history and natural textures, balancing regional character with refined perfume elegance. Moving from dark hues to lighter neutrals helped keep the design unisex—ideal for a gift any traveler can enjoy.

  • Ancient Inspiration

    Wander Northeast Scotland, and our logo may feel familiar, its form is inspired by what is commonly known Pictish sea beast, a symbol etched into the region’s stones by the early medieval Picts.

  • Packaging

    Each scent draws from a defining part of Northeast Scotland: the character of its coastal fishing towns, the calm of its windswept moors, and the strength of its granite architecture. A short description on each box highlights the fragrance notes and the inspiration behind them.

  • Stories Etched in Scent

    Each scent includes a magazine that shares its story and origins, encouraging exploration of Northeast Scotland’s overlooked gems.
