Crescent Care

UI/UX

Branding

A 2024 survey revealed that 63% of women find it difficult to prioritize their health, often due to feeling overwhelmed. Limited time and mental or emotional strain also play major roles in this challenge.

Crescent Care was created to bring calm to that chaos. By merging everyday organizational tools with specialized women’s health features, the app helps you stay informed and confident. It’s a gentle step toward feeling more grounded and empowered in your health journey.

  • The features

    On the homepage, you’ll find a daily summary of reminders, a visual showing where you are in your menstrual cycle (with the option to track or adjust it), and quick access to any recently saved articles or resources.

    The calendar lets you view your cycle history and manage tasks and reminders. Selecting a day reveals everything scheduled, where you can check items off, add new reminders, or review completed ones.

  • The Health section is your all-in-one space for medications and doctor notes. You can store contact information for your healthcare providers and set appointment reminders.

    In the Learn section, you’ll find Crescent Care’s curated, peer-reviewed articles. Anything you want to revisit can be saved to your personal library.

  • Calm & Clarity

    Soothing blues, purples, and airy tones give Crescent Care a calm, welcoming feel. The app includes only essential features for clarity and ease of use. Celestial gradients and lunar-inspired graphics nod to the connection between mood, tides, and menstrual cycles, grounding the design in natural rhythm.
