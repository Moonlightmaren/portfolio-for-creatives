Teens today are more connected than ever, yet 80% aren’t getting enough movement. Traditional workouts feel boring, exclusive, or intimidating, leaving few spaces that genuinely motivate them.

Checkpoint changes that. It’s a physical, video game–inspired park where fitness feels like adventure. Built on the same systems that make games engaging, leveling up, unlocking rewards, completing quests, and teaming up on missions, Checkpoint makes movement social, creative, and rewarding. Through bold design, and play-based progression, it transforms exercise into something teens actually want to do, a real-world checkpoint to connect, grow, and level up together
